# Build AI With Us - AI Training Curriculum

Welcome to the AI Training Curriculum repository! This project contains slides, study materials, lesson plans, and Q&A banks for teaching AI and Machine Learning to kids and teens.

This curriculum is developed in alignment with the programs offered on [Build AI With Us](https://buildaiwithus.com).

## Repository Structure

- [Curriculum Overview](curriculum_overview.md) - Details of the 10-module core curriculum and advanced tracks.
- `module_01_intro_to_ai/` - Topic: **Introduction to AI & Machine Learning** (Ages 10-13)
  - [Lesson Plan](module_01_intro_to_ai/lesson_plan.md) - Minute-by-minute teacher guide (45 mins lecture + 15 mins QnA).
  - [Slides](module_01_intro_to_ai/slides.md) - Slides deck with scripts and visual instructions.
  - [Study Material](module_01_intro_to_ai/study_material.md) - Student study packet and hands-on Google Teachable Machine activity.
  - [Q&A Bank](module_01_intro_to_ai/qna_bank.md) - Interactive solutions to student questions.

## Age Groups
- **Group 1**: 10 to 13 years old (Current focus: Module 1)
- **Group 2**: 14+ years old (Advanced/SaaS tracks)
